Wheel of Fortune

1836

In Wheel of Fortune, three pioneers compete during three rounds to solve hangman-type word puzzles. At the beginning of each round, pioneers are given a category such as a phrase, person, place or object that consists of blank spaces they must fill in with the correct letters. After random selection to see who begins, pioneers take turns to either spin the wooden wheel and guess a consonant, buy vowels from their earned winnings or solve the puzzle. At the end of three rounds, the pioneer with the most cash and prizes becomes the winner and moves to the game's bonus round.
